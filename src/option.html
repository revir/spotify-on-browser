Default keyboard shortcuts

  • Play previous track: Ctrl+shift+7 (or MediaPrevTrack on Mac)
  • Play or pause:          Ctrl+shift+8 (or MediaPlayPause on Mac)
  • Play next track:       Ctrl+shift+9 (or MediaNextTrack on Mac)
  • Save the track:        Ctrl+shift+0
